Spot the Swap

  • You will be shown a grid of food emojis. Memorise it!
  • The grid will re-appear, but some emojis will have swapped.
  • Your mission is to press on all the swapped emojis!

Well Done!

Make sure you press the red arrow on the bottom right of the screen to finish.

You will have earned an extra $2.50 for your SMART bank!

How to Play

  • Memorise the grid of food emojis.
  • When the grid comes back, find the emojis that have changed.
  • Press on all the swapped emojis to win the round!
